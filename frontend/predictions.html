Population Estimator

Gradient Boosting model trained on 100 global cities. Given a land area, it estimates population (log₁₊ₓ-transformed target) and classifies the city tier using quantile-based thresholds.

Why log-transform? City populations follow a log-normal distribution. Training in log-space converts the skewed target into an approximately Gaussian shape, dramatically improving R² from ~0.3 (raw) to ~0.6+ (log-space).
